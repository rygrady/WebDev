My Niagra Falls Vacation

Why I Went To Niagra Falls

I went to Niagra Falls during the summer before 7th grade. I went with my Dad who surprised me a week before going to the Falls. My Dad decided on
Niagra Falls because he had went there when he was my age when he went with his family. I am very grateful for him picking this destination because
it ended up being my favorite vacation. I hope that my Dad and I can return and make more memories sometime soon.

My Favorite Moments

  1. The best moment of the trip was easily the boat ride that goes super close to fall. A super fun and exciting moment for my Dad and I. The boat we were on.
  2. Eating the best slice of pizza I've ever had. I sadly don't remember the name of the place but what I do remeber is the pizza being out of this world. My Dad and I still crave this pizza to this day.
  3. Visiting the Ripley's Believe It or Not! museum. This was a fun moment because of all the weird stuff that was in the building and the unpredictability of all it.
